Short Biography of Andrea Compton I was born in Stamford, Connecticut, then moved to Palo Alto, California for a few years and then moved again to Cincinnati, Ohio where I spent the rest of my childhood. From first grade until I graduated high school I attended The Seven Hills School and became captain of the middle school and then varsity tennis team. I learned to play from my dad, and have continued playing throughout college as was on the varsity team at Tufts University. I was also recently selected for the Tufts Marathon Team and will be running in the 2015 Boston Marathon in the spring!
